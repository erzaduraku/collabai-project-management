# Sprint 1 Retrospective

> Filled at the **end** of Sprint 1, after the Sprint Review ceremony.

## Sprint #: 1

---

## What Worked Well

- GitHub Project board and sprint milestones were set up quickly and are being used consistently by the whole team.
- Communication in the team chat was clear; blockers were flagged early.
- Story definitions (acceptance criteria, story points, dependencies) were well-prepared, making planning efficient.

## What Needs Improvement

- The dependency chain starting from `ARCH-01` was too deep — it blocked 7 out of 9 stories and resulted in only 1 story being completed.
- Architecture decisions should be made (or at least drafted) before the sprint starts so they don't gate all other work.
- PR reviews need a defined turnaround time to avoid blocking teammates who depend on merged code.
